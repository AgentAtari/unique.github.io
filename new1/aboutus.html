Who we are? About USDS.

Founded in 2016, Unique Skills Domestic Services (USDS) is a registered domestic staffing agency which provides experienced, qualified and formally trained household staff including Maids, Nannies, Personal Assistants for Elderly care, Housekeepers, Domestic couples, Cooks and Drivers.

USDS caters to households of all sizes and assist families to find the right fit per their requirements.
