Answer for your Question

  • How long will it take to find a suitable candidate?

    It would typically take 1-2 week’s time to find a suitable candidate as the placement process involves initial screening and short listing of candidates from our extensive database, background verification and interview.You will be notified as soon as we identify the suitable candidates per your requirements.

  • Are all the applicants thoroughly screened before being shortlisted?

    All our applicants are subjected to thorough screening, reference checks and rigorous interview process.

  • How many hours could I expect a full time applicant to work and how many days off would I need to offer per month?

    Most applicants classify full time as 7 days a week, and prefer working to a maximum of 10 hours per day that are inclusive of breaks. As for the day offs, a minimum of 1 day is to be granted.

  • Do all childcare applicants have previous experience as nannies?

    Not necessarily, as some of the best childcares may not have worked in a home setting before but may have previously worked in a nursery (especially those holding childcare qualifications).

  • What sort of accommodation should we need to provide for Live-in candidates?

    You would need to provide as a minimum a bedroom and a bathroom.

  • Is it challenging to find a domestic help given that we live outside Hyderabad?

    Some of the candidates would be willing to relocate if the job offered suits their requirements.
    However, currently our services are only available within Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

  • What happens if I don’t like any shortlisted candidate?

    Till the time you find a suitable candidate, we will continue to provide you with the list of options to choose from.

  • What if my maid leaves after a very brief period or if I am not happy with her service?

    We offer a 12 week “like or dislike” replacement period which starts from the employee’s date of joining the employment. If you are unhappy with the services of the hired maid, we will find you a replacement free of charge.
    Please note: Our replacement policy is only applicable if our invoice has been settled as per our terms of business i.e. within 7 days from the receipt of invoice.

  • Do you do a Criminal Record Check or any other background verification on your candidates?

    Yes, we have collaborated with Janta Khoj to conduct a comprehensive background verifications of all the applicants. Only verified candidates are considered for placements.

  • When would I receive the placement invoice?

    You will receive an invoice when a candidate you decide to hire formally accepts your job offer. You are required to process the payment within 7 days from the day of receipt of the invoice.
    Please note: The invoice specifies the fees that USDS charges for helping you find the right domestic help and so, the date of joining (DOJ) of your candidate has no impact on the payment of the invoice whatsoever.

  • Who pays the domestic help every month?

    As a client you will be responsible for the payment of the salary to your hired domestic help every month.
