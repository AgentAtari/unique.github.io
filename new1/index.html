cook

Responsibilities:

  • Prepare food for the employer, their family, guests and staff.
  • Present menus for approval and discuss daily requirements with employer, house manager, housekeeper or butler.
  • Keep the kitchen in good condition, clean all surfaces and floors after use.
  • Place orders for all supplies.
  • Supervise and ensure correct presentation of all meals for functions.
  • Keep accounts for all the expenditure.
  • Supervise other kitchen staff.
  • Carry out other tasks the employer may reasonably ask.
